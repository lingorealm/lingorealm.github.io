Privacy Policy for Lingo Realm

Effective Date: 01.10.2025

Introduction

We respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your data is handled when you use our app, Lingo Realm, available on the Apple App Store.

Data Collection and Usage

We do not collect, store, or process any personal information directly. However, our app uses third-party services to serve advertisements and to collect game playing statistics.

  • Advertising: Our app uses Google AdMob and Unity Ads to serve advertisements. These services may collect data such as device information, IP address, and ad interactions to provide personalized or non-personalized ads.
  • Firebase Analytics and Database: Lingo Realm uses Firebase to collect and store game playing statistics and to construct leaderboards. Data logged includes the name of the played game, player identifier, number of hearts, purchase operations, ad interactions, and play times. This information is used solely to improve your gaming experience, analyze app usage, and manage in-app transactions.

Third-Party Services

Our app integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: Please refer to the Google Privacy Policy.
  • Unity Ads: Please refer to the Unity Privacy Policy.
  • Firebase: For details on Firebase's data practices, see the Firebase Privacy & Security documentation.

Personalized vs. Non-Personalized Ads

Personalized Ads: Ads may be tailored based on your interests inferred from your activity on other apps or websites.
Non-Personalized Ads: Ads may be delivered based solely on contextual information, such as your general location or app content.

You can manage your ad preferences or opt out of personalized ads by visiting the respective settings for Google Ads and Unity Ads.

Payment Information

If you make a purchase through the App Store, payment processing is handled solely by Apple. We do not have access to your payment information or transaction details. For more information, please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy.

Your Privacy Choices

Manage your ad preferences via the Google Ads Settings and through Unity's privacy options. You may also opt out of data collection for personalized ads where applicable.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our app, third-party integrations, or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
