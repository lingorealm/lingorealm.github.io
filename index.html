About Lingo Realm

Welcome to Lingo Realm, the ultimate vocabulary gaming app that transforms learning into an engaging adventure. With support for 10 languages and 8 exciting game modes—ranging from listening and speaking games to pairing, word-matching, translation, and quiz modes—Lingo Realm is your gateway to mastering new vocabulary for everyday use as well as exam preparation (TOEFL, GRE, and proficiency levels A1 through C1).

  • Learn vocabulary through gamification
  • 8 dynamic game modes
  • Supports 10 languages: English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Polish, Romanian, Dutch, Russian, Ukrainian
  • Exam prep modules for TOEFL, GRE, and proficiency tests
